Part 3 (40 min):

There are 7 JavaScript tasks, and you need to write code in the given editor to finish the task. You can check whether you have the right answer by clicking the given ‘Run’ button below the editor. System will show you whether your answer is right or not. You need to get the right answer to activate the ‘Next’ button to move on to your next task. All 7 tasks need to have a console log output.

In this part, you are allowed to use Google to retrieval information, and you can also use slow programming function to wait for better answers. You will see a button on the right hand side of the page once the task starts. You can click it to see different levels of answers. The waiting time is usually a few minutes. While you are waiting, you could keep using Google or coding the tasks. The answers will be displayed below the button.
