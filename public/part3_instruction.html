Part 3 (40 min):

You will be given seven (7) JavaScript tasks to complete, and you will be required to write code in the given editor to finish the task. You may check whether you have the right answer at any time by clicking the ‘Run’ button provided below the editor. The system will indicate whether or not your answer is correct. You must answer correctly in order to activate the ‘Next’ button and move on to your next task. For all seven tasks, you are required to produce console log output.

In this part, you are allowed to use Google to retrieval information, and you may also use the slow programming function to request answers related to your task. This button will be present on the right hand side of the page during each task. You can use this feature multiple times to request answers of increasing levels of quality. It may take several minutes to generate an answer. While you are waiting, you may continue searching with Google or spend the time writing code for your task. The answers will be displayed below the button.
